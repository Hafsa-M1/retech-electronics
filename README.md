# ReTech – Electronics Refurbishing and Resale Platform

**IS 3920 – Individual Project on Business Solutions**  
University of Moratuwa  
Faculty of Information Technology  
Department of Interdisciplinary Studies  

Developed by: Hafsa Mushtaq (225074N)  
Third year Individual Project – January 2026

ReTech is a full-stack web application for refurbishing and reselling used electronics. Customers submit devices → staff perform diagnostics/certification → approved items are sold online. Supports roles (Customer, Staff, Admin), orders, feedback, and notifications.

### Key Features
- Device submission & approval
- Diagnostics, certification & grading (A/B/C)
- Inventory & sales management
- Customer orders & feedback
- Notifications & basic analytics

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js
- **Backend**: Django + Django REST Framework
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
